Security Turtlebot Project

This website details the Security Bot group project from CSCI 352, Human and Robot interation/nas taught at the University of the South

Our intention is to find a simple and modern solution to increase the safety in public and private spaces.

Thus, we present the security bot: designed to patrol, protect, detect, and alert others about potential threats.

By combining sensors, programming, and a non-lethal weapon, we aim to reduce human risk and involvement in the field of security.
